## Join Our Community

Bridge Public School is a tuition-free public charter school open to all students. We welcome families who share our commitment to educational excellence.

### Who Can Apply?

### How to Apply

1. **Complete the Application**: Fill out our online application form (link coming soon)
2. **Submit Required Documents**: Proof of residency, birth certificate, immunization records
3. **Attend an Information Session**: Learn more about our school (optional but recommended)
4. **Lottery**: If applications exceed available seats, a random lottery determines enrollment
5. **Enroll**: Complete enrollment paperwork and prepare for the school year

### Lottery Information

If we receive more applications than available seats, we hold a public lottery to determine enrollment. Priority may be given to:

- Siblings of current students
- Children of staff members
- Students in our geographic priority zone

### Questions?

We're happy to answer any questions about the admissions process. Please [contact us](/contact/) or attend one of our information sessions.
